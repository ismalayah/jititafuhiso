Where did Cyrus Arnold go to high school?

Cyrus Arnold (born January 2, 2003) is an actor from the United States. He is best known for his roles as Kenny Yankovitch in Mr. Harrigan’s Phone, Derek Zoolander Jr. in Zoolander 2, BJ Malloy in Sam & Cat, Josh Jagorski in the New Line Cinema/HBO Max Original 8-Bit Christmas, and a main cast member in the Disney+ original Just Beyond.

The son of Lea Anne Wolfe and Blake Arnold, Arnold was born in Burbank, California. He has a younger brother. Arnold started acting when he was eight years old and has since appeared in a lot of television shows and commercials. He made his big-screen debut in Zoolander 2 as Derek Zoolander Jr. He has said that he would like to pursue a second career as a screenwriter. Zoolander 2 (also known as Zoolander No. 2) is a 2016 American action comedy film directed by Ben Stiller and written by John Hamburg, Justin Theroux, Stiller, and Nicholas Stoller. It is the final installment of the Zoolander series, which began in 2001. It stars Stiller, Owen Wilson, Will Ferrell, Penélope Cruz, and Kristen Wiig.
