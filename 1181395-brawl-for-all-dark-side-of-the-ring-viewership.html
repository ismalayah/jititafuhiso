Brawl For All Episode Of Dark Side Of The Ring Pulls In Over 200,000 Viewers

The latest episode of VICELAND’s Dark Side Of The Ring tackled the infamous Brawl For All from 1998 and packed quite a punch in the viewership.

According to Showbuzz Daily, the third episode of the second season of the documentary series pulled in 226,000 viewers. This number was stronger than the number pulled in for WWE Backstage, which only pulled in 140,000 viewers.

This week’s episode featured names such as Jim Cornette, Vince Russo, Droz, The Godfather, and Bart Gunn all discussing the shoot-fight tournament which Bart would go on to win.
