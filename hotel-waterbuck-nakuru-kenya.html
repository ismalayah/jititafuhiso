Hotel Waterbuck, Nakuru, Kenya

Hotel Waterbuck is located in Nakuru region of the Great Rift Valley of Kenya. We provide the best customer services that capture the heart of both local and foreign tourists. We offer no compromise on affordable quality, comfort and convenience. We have unmatched hospitality as the employees of the hotel will make you feel welcomed. The essence and experience lies in our 216 opulent Rooms and Suites, 4 Food and Beverage outlets, swimming pool and a decadent health club combined into four elements of luxuries comfort, the wild and the wind. Business amenities include a 24-hr business Centre, technology support staff and audio- visual equipment, making the hotel perfect for events and conferences. Not only giving our guests a lust view of tranquility and peace will also guarantee unrivaled security to all our guests. Drum your body into shape at our state of the art Waterbuck health club or sit by the poolside bar whilst trying to sample your favorite brand. We also offer our guests aerobics, swimming pool training.
