Daniel Peter Masterson is an American actor who is best known for his role as Steven Hyde in That ’70s Show.

He belongs to a family of actors with three of his siblings having successful careers in the acting industry. This article will focus on his four siblings.

Meet Christopher Masterson, Alanna Masterson, Jordan Masterson and Will Masterson, Danny Masterson’s siblings

Chris Masterson was born to Carol and Peter Masterson. His father is an insurance agent and his mother is a manager. His parents got divorced and his mother remarried Joe Reiche, a former Australian rugby player.

He has a younger brother named  Christopher who is best known for playing the role of Francis on Malcolm in the Middle. He has two maternal half-siblings from his mother’s second marriage, named Alanna Masterson and Jordan Masterson.

Both Alanna and Jordan are active in the acting industry like Masterson. He also has a paternal half brother named Will.
